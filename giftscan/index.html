Privacy Policy

GiftScan — Last updated: April 2026

This privacy policy describes how the GiftScan app ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information to our servers. Specifically:

  • Wishlists and items are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. They never leave your device unless you explicitly share them.
  • Barcode scans are sent to a product lookup proxy to retrieve product names, descriptions, and prices. Only the barcode number is transmitted — no user identity or location is included.
  • Camera access is used solely for scanning barcodes and QR codes. No images are saved or transmitted.

Barcode Lookups

When you scan a barcode, the app queries a backend proxy (hosted on Cloudflare Workers) which looks up the product across several free public databases (eBay, UPCitemdb, Google Books, and others). The barcode number may be cached server-side for up to 30 days to improve performance for all users. No personal data is associated with these cache entries.

Sharing

Wishlists are only shared when you explicitly choose to share them (e.g. via Messages, Mail, or AirDrop). We have no access to your contacts or shared content.

Amazon Affiliate Links

Product links in the app may include Amazon affiliate tracking tags. Clicking these links takes you to Amazon's website. We may earn a small commission on qualifying purchases at no extra cost to you. Amazon's own privacy policy applies to any interactions on their platform.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services for product lookups. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • eBay
  • Google (Books API)
  • UPCitemdb

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
