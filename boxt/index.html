Privacy Policy

BOXT — Last updated: April 2026

BOXT is a smart vehicle packing assistant that helps you organize and optimize how you load items into your vehicle. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

BOXT collects only what is necessary to provide its core features:

  • Camera & Photos: BOXT uses your device camera to photograph individual items so the app can classify them and suggest dimensions. Photos are processed entirely on your device using Apple's Vision framework and are never uploaded to any server.
  • Item Data: Information you enter about your items (name, dimensions, weight, fragility) is stored locally on your device using SwiftData. This data never leaves your device.
  • No Account Required: BOXT requires no account, login, or registration. We collect no personal identifying information.
  • No Analytics: We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.
  • No Advertising: BOXT contains no ads and shares no data with advertising networks.

Augmented Reality

BOXT uses ARKit to display an augmented reality overlay of your cargo space. AR processing happens entirely on your device. No images or video from your camera feed are stored or transmitted.

Third-Party Services

BOXT uses the following Apple frameworks, all of which operate entirely on-device:

  • Vision (Apple): Used to classify photographed items. All inference is on-device.
  • ARKit / RealityKit (Apple): Used for augmented reality packing guidance.
  • SceneKit (Apple): Used for 3D packing visualization.
  • SwiftData (Apple): Used for local on-device data persistence.

No data is sent to Apple or any other third party as a result of using these frameworks within BOXT.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any of your data with third parties. All data remains on your device at all times.

Children's Privacy

BOXT does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. The app does not collect personal information from any user.

Data Retention & Deletion

All data created within BOXT is stored locally on your device. You can delete all app data at any time by deleting the app from your device. We have no access to your data and cannot delete it on your behalf.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
